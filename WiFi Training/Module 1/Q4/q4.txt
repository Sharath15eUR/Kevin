Q4) What is the main functionality of a wifi accesspoint?

A wifi accesspoint converts the information from a wired network into a wireless information. It enables a wired device to connect to a wireless medium and allow other wireless devices to communicate with said wired device.
It is also responsible for acting as a signal extender, and also helps in network management(firewalls, device management, etc.).
