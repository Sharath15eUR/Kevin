Q) In which OSI layer does WIFI standard/protocol fits?

Ans) WiFi comes under both Physical and Data link layer. WiFi involves with transmission of data through wireless means (radio waves), bands (2.5GHz/5GHz), etc. This comes under the Physical layer.
Wifi also is part of data link due to the addressing, multiplexing, CSMA/CD (error detection and control).
