Q3) What is BSS and ESS?

BSS (Basic Service Set):
  It refers to a group of wireless devices that can communicate with each other. Essentially, it is a WLAN network. For example, a home wireless network is a BSS.
ESS (Extended Service Set):
  It refers to a group of BSS that are interconnected with each other. For example, an office with multiple WLAN networks interconnected consists of an ESS.
