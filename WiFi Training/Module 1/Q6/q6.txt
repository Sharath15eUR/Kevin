Q6) Difference between 802.11a and 802.11b

802.11a:
  i) Operates in 5GHz band
  ii) Offers higher datarate (54Mbps)
  iii) Shorter range and prone to obstacles like walls

802.11b:
  i) Operates in 2.4GHz band
  ii) Offers lower datarate (11Mbps)
  iii) Longer range and is not affected by obstacles like walls
